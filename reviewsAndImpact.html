Pop Culture References

Fortnite has made an undeniable impact on pop culture. From viral dances like the Floss to collaborations with global franchises, Fortnite has transcended its identity as just a video game.

  • Collaborations with Marvel, DC, Star Wars, Dragon Ball Z, and even Ariana Grande.
  • Virtual concerts by Travis Scott and Marshmello, attended by millions live in-game.
  • Popular emotes like "Orange Justice" and "Take the L" became real-life dance crazes.
  • Referenced in TV shows like *Stranger Things*, *The Simpsons*, and *South Park*.
  • Even NFL teams and major celebrities have played and streamed the game!
